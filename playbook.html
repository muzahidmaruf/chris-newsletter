Why This, Why Now

The demographic cliff didn't create these problems. It exposed them.

In July 2026, TIME made the case that higher ed's business model is breaking — the enrollment cliff, federal funding cuts, new caps on student loans. Most of that is outside your control. What you can control is how your institution responds. This playbook won't fix your business model. It will help you stop losing the ground you never had to lose:

A market you could have won

A territory, transfer partnership, or adult-learner segment another institution captured while you were still in committee.

A person you lost

A high performer who burned out waiting on clarity, decision rights, or any sign of forward movement.

A student experience you compromised

A confusing portal, a delayed message, a siloed handoff that made students feel like they were navigating five universities.

A retention fix you delayed

A process change that would have removed friction for students, pushed again to "next cycle."
